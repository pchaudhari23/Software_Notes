GENARATIVE AI:

Prompt techniques:

1. Zero shot: No examples
2. Few shot: Guided examples
3. Chain of thought: Step wise reasoning
4. Role prompting: Assigned persona
5. Instruction prompting: Clear tasks
6. Contextual prompting: Added background
7. Constraint prompting: Rules/ format limits
8. Multi step reasoning: Sequential guidance

==============================================================

* Be clear and specific
* Provide context
* Role prompting + Chain of thought
* Select correct agent and mode
* Ask for justification/ multiple options
* Avoid blanket statement prompts for large codebases
* Tell what not to do
* Step back approach for bug fixing
* Prompt formula = Role + Context + Task + Constraints + Output
